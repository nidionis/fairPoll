Quick & Simple Voting

  • Everyday Decisions: For informal voting, use new quickpoll and share the poll ID with your participants.
  • Important Decisions: Use new quickpoll and select ticket secure.
  • Idea: For several votes from the same device, use private navigation.

Secure House Voting

Regular decisions: We recommend our secure House voting system (sending automatic emails).

How it works:

  1. Gather your voters: All voters must belong to the House.
  2. Create a house: Set it up with all its voting members.
    Note: To prevent ballot stuffing, you cannot add new voters after the House is created.
  3. Create the Poll: Initiate a new poll from within the House.
  4. Distribute Tickets: The poll creator must print, cut out, and randomly distribute the voting tickets (for example, by having members draw them from a box).
  5. Cast Votes: Each voter joins the poll using the poll ID, and uses their unique ticket to cast their vote anonymously (no sign-in required when voting a ticket poll).
  6. Review Results: Once voting ends, the results are stored in the House archives. The complete ballot can be downloaded for independent verification.
