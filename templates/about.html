About FairPoll

Welcome.

For a quick vote, or for decisions that do not require a high level of importance, use QuickPoll and share the poll ID.

To ensure a properly secured vote and reduce issues such as ballot stuffing, mistakes, and server-side monitoring concerns, please follow the instructions below:

  1. Voters must belong to the house.
  2. Create the house.
    Note: you cannot add a voter after creating a house, in order to avoid ballot stuffing.
  3. Create the poll through the house.
  4. The creator must print, cut, and randomly distribute the tickets (for example, using a hat).
  5. Each voter uses their ticket to vote.
  6. At the end of the vote, it is stored in the house archives, and the ballot can be downloaded for verification.

Note: in the event of a tie, the order of the propositions matters.

The group is free to invalidate a vote according to its own rules (tie, ballot corruption, and so on).
