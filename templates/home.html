Security concept

The distribution of unique physical tokens is handled by you (random draw): the server cannot link a ballot to a person, ensuring the vote is anonymous.

The results are published and verifiable: even the most skeptical can confirm how their vote is counted, as well as the calculations.

Group Autonomy

FairPoll provides the tools, but your group remains in control. The group is entirely free to invalidate a vote according to its own agreed-upon rules (such as in the case of ties or ballot corruption).
